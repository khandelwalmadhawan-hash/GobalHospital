About Us

Global Super Speciality Hospital, Mumbai (a unit of Global Healthcare Systems), is a multidisciplinary 350 bedded tertiary care hospital providing comprehensive medical care across all major specialities. Our patient-centric approach blended with advanced technology helps our clinicians deliver superior clinical outcomes in all disciplines. Experts at Global Hospital have successfully treated more than 50 lakh patients across 45 specialties, including Cardiac Surgery, Advanced Neurosciences, Urology, Orthopaedics, Organ Transplant, Oncology, and Women's Health.

We are committed to delivering the highest standards of healthcare excellence and are continually investing in world-class infrastructure and training.
